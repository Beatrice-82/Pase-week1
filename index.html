About Us

Vadvians Kitchen is a Limited Liability Company incorporated on 20th of September, 2018. Vadvians Kitchenstarted operation with a workforce of 5 in 2017 but the number increased to 20. It offered job opportunities for the native of Kumasi. Vadvians Kitchen gives an irrestible taste that you can trust. Vadvians Kitchen has the distinction of being the pioneer of te Fast Food Restaurants in Kumasi. over the years Vadvians Kitchen has kept faith with a strategy which was to prepare dishies according to customer prefrence wich gives their food a unique taste.
